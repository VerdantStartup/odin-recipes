Mac n' Cheese

As you can see we have some very yummy noodels baked wit cheese

Somewhat of a traditional american dish

Ingredients

  • 4 spoons of butter
  • 2 spoons of flour
  • 375 ml of milk
  • 180g of cheese
  • 2 spoons of parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the noodels according to the package instructions
  2. Add the butter, flour and milk
  3. Add the cheese and wait until it's molten
  4. When it's done put everything into the pot of your choice
  5. Put it into the oven for 30 to 40 minuets
